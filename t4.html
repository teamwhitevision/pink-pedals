Discover Jaipur
On Bicycles
OhMyJaipur


Feeling of being a King as you pedal through real India, Real Jaipur and Farmers land. One delightful experience you should carry yourself from one of its kind Village Tour


RIDE LEVEL - Normal Ride


PRICE Rs 4000 /Person up to 5 people, Rs 3500 /Person for more than 5 people


AREAS COVERED - Jal Mahal. Amer Fort, Sagar Lake, Close look of Aravalli Hills, One village near by Jaipur.


ITINARY

1. Receiving guests from their hotels.
2. Assembling & Introduction at Sanganeri Gate, briefing about the ride, safety points and cycle gears. s
3. Feel the cool breeze at small stop over Jal Mahal (water palace) and relax to gear up for the fascinating journey of king’s era.
4. As you pedal, you will reach to the landmark of Jaipur heritage, The Amer fort. Enjoy the outer side of fort and move forward to see some amazing places like, Sagar Lake, Panna Heera ka kund (Step Well) etc. 5. Relax & enjoy packed snacks with seasonal fruits and packed juices, local tea.
5. Starting pedalling and head towards a village.
6. Real Village Ride starts as you pedal across the muddy terrain and cobbled uneven path full of stones and fresh winds. People dressed up in their regular but ethnic Rajasthani dresses. Mud houses, green lush agricultural farms, house of a potter and what not will make you understand Rajasthan very closely.
7.Breakfast in local’s house or at some famous stop (Depend on Availability) and leisure time and heading towards one beautiful surprise location of the tour for clicking pictures
8.Dropping off cycles and ride back towards respective hotel in car.

2. Mobile Pouch.
3. Mineral water bottles.
4. Breakfast and tea/coffee/juice with local food tasting.
5. Multi-lingual Guide and an Escort (for taking care of riders) .
6. Lock and key.
7. Backup vehicle/cycles.
