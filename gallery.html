FAQ

1. How can I rent a cycle?

Walk down at Jawahar Circle and choose amongst the fleet of cycles we offer by either depositing your Original Photo Address Proof or Rs 1000 which will be refunded once the cycle is returned to us after making the full payment.

2. How should I pay money?

You can make payment through cash or money transfer thru e-wallets like Paytm, cash..

3. What if owner demands some ID proof?

Yes, as a SECURITY we certainly require your ORIGINAL Photo Address Proof like AADHAR, Voter ID.

4. What are valid ID proofs?

5. What is the minimum time period of hire?

There is no minimum time period while you rent a cycle with Pink Pedals

6. Is there a contract? What are the terms?

Yes, as you agree to rent a cycle and Pink Pedals agrees to it then there is a mutual contract where,

  • a. Pink Pedals is bind to give a cycle available on rent as per the choice made by the client on providing necessary documents or security amount.
  • b. Client of Pink Pedals is bind to pay the security amount or documents (as required) and complete rental charge
  • c. Cycle should be returned in the same condition as it was when handling it.
  • d. Pink Pedals do not owe any responsibility against any harm occurred during the rental period. It will be solely responsibility of the client.
7. When do I get my refundable deposit back?

When the client returns the rented cycle, security amount or any documents submitted will be refunded.

8. Who handles the delivery?

We have trained and educated staff which co-ordinates in the delivery of cycles amongst the clients of Pink Pedals.

9. How safety is insured?

Pink Pedals aim at proper security of its client while they feel the magic of Mother Nature and aroma of good health. For this we provide Helmets free of cost with all our cycles.
