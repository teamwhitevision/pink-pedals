PINK Aura


Be on Saddle & Pink Aura will bring Jaipur more close to you, visit all from world famous heritage sites to the local Jaipur, The real Pink city (Gulabi Shahar).


PRICE Rs 1300 / Person up to 5 people, Rs 1100 / Person for more than 5 people


RIDE LEVEL - Normal 6-8 Kms on road basic riding and 1 km of regular walk


AREAS COVERED - Local Streets, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Govind Devji Temple, Flower Market, Local food streets.


Meeting Point- Raj Mandir Cinema Hall, Panch Batti Circle


RIDE LEVEL - Normal 6-8 Kms on road basic riding and 1 km of regular walk


ITINARY

1. Assembling & Introduction at Sanganeri Gate, briefing about the ride, safety points.
2. A morning with Wind Palace, The Hawa Mahal
3. Jaleb Chowk, the centre of City Palace .Pedalling our wheels through the lanes and getting a sneak peak of real Jaipur. Tour starts after offering prayers to Hindu God Krishna at a famous Govind Devji Temple.
4. Park your cycles at a decided point. Will walk forward and visit the early morning wholesale vegetable and flower market.
5. Local food tasting all through your trip will certainly relish your taste buds.
6. Its breakfast time, have some famous delicacies and tea.
7. Will move forward to our starting point to conclude our Hidden Pink City Tour
