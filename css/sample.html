Our Concept

Pink Pedals, where cycling is a habit, is a bold initiative towards society in which we are working to provide all age groups a better idea of how to be fit, healthy and happy with minimum efforts. Pink Pedals is for everyone, men, women, and children.

Pink Pedals in a unique and new concept to promote cycling, we provide good quality, high end geared and Non-geared cycles on rent. We are specialised in organising corporate cycling events, Cyclothons, Customised Cycling city tours.

We also work with corporates to ensure wellness and healthy living of employee’s. We promote Cycle with Work with our hashtag #CycletoWork #CycleWithWork

Our Vision

Our Vision is our commitment towards betterment of our environment, our people.

We aim at promoting Sustainable mode of Mobility and prioritising old, but yet a new way of commutation and leisure i.e. CYCLES

Founder

The mind behind the concept is Ms Pooja Vijay who is a Commerce Graduate from reputed educational institute Kanoria Girls College. She also completed her Law from Rajasthan University.

She worked by giants MNCs like Genpact and Infosys earlier.

Pooja Vijay, an young entrepreneur and social activist who founded Pink Pedals in 2017, the Rajasthan’s first and largest cycle rental and bike sharing services with the vision of promoting bicycles. She was also chosen as first “Bicycle Mayor of Jaipur” by Amsterdam based organisation BYCS working on a mission of 50by30 means 50 per cent of city rides on cycle by 2030.
